It is always a pleasure for me to talk about the library of the Polytechnic Museum. This is a wonderful, unique collection of books, brochures, magazines, manuscripts, many of which have survived only here, sometimes in a single copy. All this wealth is in the care of a charming team of professionals and enthusiasts in librarianship. The library has a rich history. It allows you to identify a number of stages in the activities of this collection of scientific and technical literature.
